What is GCD ?
Greatest Common Divisor (GCD) or Highest Common Factor (HCF) is the 
largest integer that divides the number without a remainder.

FORMULA TO FIND GCD OF TWO NO.s =  (a*b)/LCM

What is LCM ?
Least Common Multiple (LCM) is the smallest positive integer that
is the multiple of all the given numbers.

FORMULA FOR LCM OF TWO Numbers = (a*b)/GCD
